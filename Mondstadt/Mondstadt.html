Mondstadt – The land of Freedom

A land where the winds carry songs of liberty, Mondstadt is a realm unbound by kings or tyrants, where freedom is the heart of its people. Vast green fields, rolling hills, and crystal-clear lakes define its landscape, crowned by the towering peaks of Dragonspine and the grandeur of the City of Wind. At its core stands Mondstadt itself, a lively city where bards sing, knights uphold honor, and citizens live without chains. The Anemo Archon, watches over his people not as a ruler, but as a guiding wind, believing that true strength comes from the unshackled spirit. Though storms may rise and threats may loom, Mondstadt endures, carried forward by the unwavering will of those who cherish the wind’s embrace.

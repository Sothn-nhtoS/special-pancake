Fontaine - The Land of Justice

A land where the rhythm of the law governs all. Known for its beautiful canals, grand architecture, and elegant waterways, Fontaine is a place where innovation and tradition coexist in harmony. The people of Fontaine hold justice in the highest regard, and their society is deeply rooted in a belief in fairness and truth. The city is powered by the magnificent power of Hydro, with great machines and water-driven mechanisms that keep the land flowing. At its core is the Hydro Archon, a being of great wisdom and grace, whose influence ensures that the laws of Fontaine are enforced with unwavering precision. However, beneath the surface of this seemingly perfect society, secrets lie hidden, and not all is as just as it seems.

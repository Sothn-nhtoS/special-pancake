Liyue – The Land of Contracts

A land where stone and commerce stand firm, Liyue is a testament to the endurance of tradition and the strength of unwavering contracts. Carved between towering mountains and tranquil rivers, its landscapes are steeped in history, every cliff and peak echoing the presence of gods and legends. At its heart lies Liyue Harbor, the thriving economic center of Teyvat, where merchants and travelers gather in pursuit of prosperity. The people of Liyue, industrious and pragmatic, honor their agreements with diligence, believing that stability is forged through trust and hard work. Overseeing this balance is the Geo Archon,the God of Contracts, whose wisdom shaped the foundations of the nation. Though change stirs in the wind, Liyue endures—its legacy set in stone, unyielding to the passage of time.

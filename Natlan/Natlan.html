Natlan - The Land of Fire

A realm where the very earth burns with the fury of an eternal flame. Bathed in heat and fervor, its landscape is carved by towering volcanoes and vast deserts, their endless sands swirling like embers in the wind.The people of Natlan are as passionate as the fire they revere, their culture rich with vibrant festivals, fierce music, and fiery dance. At the heart of this land stands the Pyro Archon, a deity who commands the flames and protects the land with unwavering strength. Though the fires of Natlan rage fiercely, its people live in harmony with their harsh yet beautiful surroundings, their spirits as untamed as the fire that courses through the land.
